The Impossible List or as I like to call it ‘I’m Possible List’

I initially read about the idea of an Impossible List on Thomas Frank's blog, College Info Geek. He discovered the idea through Joel Runyon, who made the world's first impossible list and characterized the distinction between it and a regular ‘ole bucket list.

“Put simply, the impossible list is an ever-evolving list of experiences that build upon each other, help others as well as yourself, and implore you to take action.” - Thomas Frank
I’ve always been a firm believer in goal setting in order to get the most out of life, so soon after discovering the concept, I decided to create an Impossible List of my own. For my list I have amassed goals and experiences that are impossible to do without effectively taking a shot at them. I have broken down the list into manageable categories. Although this list is quite personal to me and tailored to my life, I am posting it online with the hopes that it will keep me more accountable and force me to follow through. I want to experience as much as I can in this lifetime and hope you do too!
