Day 1
Sat, Jun 27
🚗 ~7–8 hrs total with boating detour

Milpitas → Lake Britton → Medford

Hotel / base: Medford — Quality Inn & Suites Medford Airport. Check-in 3 PM / check-out 11 AM. Free breakfast. $150 security/deposit hold.

How to run this day

  • Leave Milpitas around 7:30–8:00 AM if you want the boating stop.
  • Lunch + Tesla charge around Red Bluff or Redding.
  • Keep the day flexible; this is still mostly a positioning day.
  • If late, skip boating and go straight to Medford/Ashland.
Key caveat: Lake Britton boating depends on marina hours, weather, and availability. Keep it to ~1 hour so you do not reach Medford too late.

🚗 ~4–5 hrs driving + viewpoints

Medford → Rogue Gorge → Natural Bridge → Crater Lake → Bend

Hotel / base: Bend — TownePlace Suites by Marriott Bend. Check-in 4 PM / check-out 12 PM. Free breakfast. Full kitchen. Prepaid/non-refundable.

How to run this day

  • Leave Medford around 7:30–8:30 AM.
  • Do Rogue Gorge + Natural Bridge before Crater Lake.
  • Prioritize rim viewpoints; no need to over-hike this day.
  • Grocery run in Bend for WFH days.
Key caveat: Crater Lake weather can be windy/cold even when sunny. Carry windcheater or jacket. Lake access/boat tours are not part of this plan.

Hotel / base: Bend — TownePlace Suites by Marriott Bend. WFH base with kitchen/desk.

Day 7
Fri, Jul 3
🚗 ~5 hrs driving + many coastal stops

Hillsboro → North & Central Oregon Coast → Florence

Hotel / base: Florence — Quality Inn Florence. Check-in 4 PM / check-out 11 AM. Free breakfast. Room: 1 King, non-smoking.

How to run this day

  • Start early; this is one of the densest days.
  • Cannon Beach and Ecola are core; Hug Point depends on tide.
  • Cape Perpetua/Thor’s Well and Heceta are the end-of-day priorities.
  • Cut food/creamery/minor stops if running late.
Key caveat: Coast is windy/cool and parking can be slow. Do not stand too close to Thor’s Well or slick rocks.
